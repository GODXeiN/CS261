FAQ


Navigation
How do I navigate to my projects?

You first have to create a project by clicking "Create New Project". After you complete the form you can view it on the left column of the home page. Simply click on the project you wish to observe and you will be redirected in the view project page.

Risk Assessment
How is the risk assessment generated?

We trained a Machine Learning model to classify projects into Successes and Failures, using research provided by past Software-Engineering projects. Data Science + Magic = Risk Assessment.

How often can I refresh the risk assessment generator?

You can press "Calculate Risk" anytime you wish. This will take into account any changes you have made since the last calculation.

Editting Metrics
What is edit project details?

This is the page where you can make changes to the project details. Even those you have already set up such as the deadline and the budget. This will change the result of your risk assessment.

How do I link a repository?

When you enter a GitHub token and a GitHub URL, we validate them and then we get access to your repository.

What is the survey interval?

Regular surveys will be sent to your developers' email address the answers of which will be taken into consideration for the risk assessment. The survey interval value defines (in days) how often a survey will be sent out to your team.

Other Options
How do I add developers?

You simply add the developer's email address into the field on "Manage Developers". After that, you can immediately see them in the table on the right.

What are internal deadlines?

In a software project, you should assign smaller tasks with deadlines within the final deadline of your project. You can set those up in "Internal Deadlines" by entering their title and deadline. After that, you can view them in the table on the right as well as declariung them successful or failed at any point.
